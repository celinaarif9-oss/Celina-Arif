# Launch Playbook — collection drops

A repeatable routine so every collection launches the same clean way: **tease → launch →
sustain** — captions pre-written, nothing forgotten on drop day. Copy this per drop and fill the
brief.

**Replaces:** scrambling on launch day and improvising captions/emails each time.

---

## Launch brief (fill per drop)
- **Collection name:** ___
- **Drop date & time:** ___ (suggest **8–9pm PKT** — catches PK evening + UK daytime)
- **No. of pieces / price range:** ___
- **Hero images chosen:** ___ (creative = founder)

---

## Pre-launch checklist (finish 2–3 days before)
Tick every box before you tease — a smooth drop depends on it.

**Product & store**
- [ ] Pieces loaded on Shopify, **priced** (from the Cost Card → Pricing Calculator)
- [ ] **Photography** final (hero + detail shots)
- [ ] **Sizes XXS–XXL + "Add custom measurements"** showing on each product (Globo)
- [ ] Each product has a **weight set** (Pret ~1kg, Formals ~2kg) so shipping rates fire
- [ ] Descriptions include the **customisation options** allowed

**Behind the scenes (already built — just confirm ON)**
- [ ] **Shipping rates** live (Rs.500 local / DHL international)
- [ ] **Abandoned-cart** email automation ON
- [ ] **Payment methods** working (card / bank transfer / Western Union)
- [ ] **Quick replies + FAQ** loaded (you'll get a question spike — be ready)
- [ ] Production team briefed on expected **made-to-order volume**

**Content ready**
- [ ] Teaser, launch & sustain **captions** picked (below)
- [ ] **Shopify Email** teaser + launch emails drafted (below)
- [ ] Posts **scheduled** in Meta Business Suite (IG + FB)

---

## The timeline

| When | Channel | Action |
|------|---------|--------|
| **T‑7** | IG/FB post + Story | **Teaser** — hint, no reveal |
| **T‑3** | Story | Behind-the-scenes / fabric close-up |
| **T‑1** | IG/FB post + Story + **Email** | **Countdown** — "tomorrow" |
| **Launch day (T)** | IG/FB post + Story + **Email** | **Launch** — it's live |
| **T+1** | Story | Repost first **#wearC** / detail shots |
| **T+3** | IG/FB post + Story | **Sustain** — "going fast / final sizes" |

## Captions (in your voice — warm, minimal)

**T‑7 Teaser**
- `Something new is coming.`
- `A new edit, this [day].`

**T‑1 Countdown**
- `Tomorrow. [Collection].`
- `[Collection] — live tomorrow, [time].`

**Launch day**
- `[Collection] is here.`
- `Say hello to [Collection]. Now available.`
- `[Collection] is live — made to order, in your size.`

**T+3 Sustain**
- `Going fast — a few sizes left in [piece].`
- `Still available: [piece]. Made to order.`

**#wearC repost (Story)**
- `[First name] in [piece]. #wearC`

## Emails (Shopify Email — your voice)

**T‑1 Teaser email**
- Subject: `Something new is coming`
- Body: `A new edit arrives tomorrow at [time]. We can't wait to show you. — Celina Arif`

**Launch email**
- Subject: `[Collection] is here`
- Body: `[Collection] is now available — made to order, in your size. Explore the edit. [Shop link]`

---

## Launch-day go-live checklist (the 5-minute final pass)
- [ ] Products **published** / set to active
- [ ] Open the store in **incognito** — prices, sizes, "add measurements", add-to-cart all work
- [ ] Do a **test checkout** to the cart step — shipping shows correctly (local + one intl)
- [ ] Launch **post live** + **Story** up + **email sent**
- [ ] Team watching **WhatsApp/Instagram** for the question spike (quick replies ready)

---

## After the drop
- Repost **#wearC** customer photos to Stories (free social proof).
- Note which pieces sold fastest → tells you what to make more of.
- Once the **KPI dashboard** is live, log the drop's revenue/units to compare launches.

## How to run it
Copy this file per collection, fill the brief, work the checklist top to bottom. Creative
(images, collection story) stays with the founder; everything else is a tick-box the team can run.
