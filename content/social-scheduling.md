# Social scheduling SOP

Plan and schedule a week of posts in one sitting, so the feed stays consistent without daily
effort — and without relying on Pitch to post.

**Replaces:** ad-hoc posting (and Pitch running the calendar).

## Tool: Meta Business Suite (free)

You're already on Meta, and Business Suite schedules **Instagram + Facebook together** at no
cost — no need for a paid app unless you later want deeper analytics (Later/Metricool).

- Desktop: **business.facebook.com** → **Planner** (or the **Create post** → **Schedule**).
- Phone: **Meta Business Suite** app → **Planner**.

## Cadence & mix

**3 posts/week** (more in launch/festive weeks). A simple rotation that stays minimal:

| | Post | Type |
|---|------|------|
| Mon | New arrival / hero piece | feed |
| Wed | Craft / behind-the-scenes or made-to-order | feed |
| Fri | Restock, customer feature (#wearC), or teaser | feed |

Plus **Stories** as you like (repost #wearC customer photos, quick BTS) — these can be casual.

## Weekly routine (~20 min, once a week)

1. Pick **3 images** with the founder (creative stays yours).
2. For each, choose a caption from `caption-templates.md` (or generate with the AI prompt) →
   **you approve it**.
3. In Business Suite **Planner → Create post** → add image + caption → select **Instagram +
   Facebook** → **Schedule** for the day/time.
4. Done for the week.

**Best times** (Pakistan audience, test and adjust): evenings ~8–10pm PKT; for the big
international audience (UK/US), late PK night also catches UK daytime.

## Approval rule

Captions can be **drafted by AI**, but a person **approves and schedules** every post — it's
public publishing. Nothing auto-posts. (Same human-in-the-loop principle as customer messages.)

## Light monthly check

Once the KPI dashboard is live, glance at which posts drove profile visits / DMs and lean into
those formats. No need for heavy analytics yet.
