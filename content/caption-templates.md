# Caption library

Warm, friendly, and minimal — on-voice captions by post type. Fill the `[bracket]`, pick a
line, post. Read `brand-voice.md` first. Keep them short — friendly, never wordy.

> Replaces: waiting on Pitch to write captions.

## New arrival
- `Say hello to [piece name].`
- `[Piece name] is here — now available.`
- `New in, and made just for you. [Piece name].`

## Launch / teaser
- `Something new is on its way.`
- `A little something new, this [day].`
- `Can't wait to show you — coming [day].`

## Restock
- `You asked — [piece name] is back.`
- `Good news: [piece name] is available again.`

## Behind the scenes / craft
- `A peek inside the atelier.`
- `Made by hand, with care.`
- `Every piece, stitched to your measurements.`

## Made-to-order / customisation
- `Made just for you, in your size. DM to begin.`
- `Yours, made to measure — message us anytime.`

## Festive / Eid
- `Dressed and ready for the season.`
- `Your Eid look — [piece name].`

## Limited / final pieces (use sparingly)
- `Just a few left, if it's calling you.`
- `Final sizes — here while they last.`

## Customer feature (UGC)
- `Loved seeing [first name] in [piece]. #wearC`
- `Wearing Celina Arif? Tag #wearC — we'd love to share it.`
- `Looks beautiful on you. #wearC`

## A light Roman-Urdu touch (optional, occasional)
- `Bilkul aap ke liye. Made to order.`
- `Eid ki tayari shuru? [Piece name], now available.`
*(Use rarely, only when it feels natural.)*

## Generate more on demand (AI prompt)
Paste this into Claude/ChatGPT with the piece details to get fresh options in-voice:

```
Write 3 Instagram caption options for Celina Arif, a luxury made-to-order Pakistani
womenswear label.
Voice: elegant, warm, friendly, and minimal — like Aleen Sabbagh but a touch more
inviting. No hype, no extra information, no pressure. 1–2 short lines max. Mostly English,
with a light optional touch of Roman Urdu only if it feels natural. Emoji rare. Soft,
welcoming CTA only (e.g. "Now available", "DM to begin").
Piece: [name]. Fabric/detail: [e.g. hand-embroidered organza]. Occasion: [e.g. festive].
Post type: [new arrival / restock / teaser / BTS / festive].
```

Then **you pick and approve** one before it's scheduled.
