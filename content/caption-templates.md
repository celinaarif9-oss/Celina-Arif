# Caption library

Minimal, on-voice captions by post type. Fill the `[bracket]`, pick a line, post. Read
`brand-voice.md` first. Keep them short — if in doubt, cut a word.

> Replaces: waiting on Pitch to write captions.

## New arrival
- `[Piece name].`
- `[Piece name] — now available.`
- `New in. [Piece name], made to order.`

## Launch / teaser
- `Something new, soon.`
- `A new edit, this [day].`
- `Coming [day].`

## Restock
- `Back by request — [piece name].`
- `[Piece name], available again.`

## Behind the scenes / craft
- `In the atelier.`
- `Made by hand.`
- `Every piece, in your measurements.`

## Made-to-order / customisation
- `Made to order in your size. DM to begin.`
- `Yours, made to measure.`

## Festive / Eid
- `Dressed for the season.`
- `Eid, in [piece name].`

## Limited / final pieces (use sparingly)
- `A few pieces remain.`
- `Final sizes available.`

## Customer feature (UGC)
- `[First name] in [piece]. #wearC`
- `Tag #wearC to be featured.`
- `Seen on you. #wearC`

## A light Roman-Urdu touch (optional, occasional)
- `Bilkul aap ke liye. Made to order.`
- `Eid ki tayari. [Piece name], now available.`
*(Use rarely, only when it feels natural.)*

---

## Generate more on demand (AI prompt)
Paste this into Claude/ChatGPT with the piece details to get fresh options in-voice:

```
Write 3 Instagram caption options for Celina Arif, a luxury made-to-order Pakistani
womenswear label.
Voice: elegant, understated, friendly, minimal — like Aleen Sabbagh. No hype, no extra
information, no pressure. 1–2 short lines max; often just the piece name works. Mostly
English, with a light optional touch of Roman Urdu only if it feels natural. Emoji rare.
Soft CTA only (e.g. "Now available", "DM to order").
Piece: [name]. Fabric/detail: [e.g. hand-embroidered organza]. Occasion: [e.g. festive].
Post type: [new arrival / restock / teaser / BTS / festive].
```

Then **you pick and approve** one before it's scheduled.
