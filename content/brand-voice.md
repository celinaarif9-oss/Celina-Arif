# Celina Arif — Brand voice (social)

The guide for every caption, story, and reply. Keep it here; update it when the voice shifts.

## Personality
Elegant, warm, friendly, quietly confident. The garment is the hero — words stay out of its way,
but they say hello. Think **Aleen Sabbagh**: minimal and considered, with a welcoming touch.

## The voice in one line
> Say it warmly, say it short. Let the piece speak. Friendly, never salesy.

## Rules
- **Short.** One line, occasionally two. Often just the piece name is enough.
- **No extra information.** No fabric essays, no backstory unless it's genuinely one line.
- **No hype.** Avoid "amazing", "must-have", "obsessed", "🔥", multiple "!!!", or pressure
  ("hurry", "last chance"). Luxury doesn't shout.
- **Friendly, not fussy.** Warm and human, never gushing or "too loving."
- **Mostly English.** A light touch of **Roman Urdu** occasionally, only when it adds warmth
  (e.g. a soft sign-off) — never forced.
- **Emoji: rare.** Default to none. At most one tasteful mark, occasionally.

## Hashtags & handle
- Instagram: **@celinaariff**
- **#wearC** — the brand/customer tag. Invite customers to use it when they post; repost the
  best to Stories. Keep it out of most brand captions (it's *theirs*).
- Keep discovery hashtags minimal — if used, place them in the **first comment**, not the caption.

## Calls to action (soft only)
- "Now available."  ·  "Made to order — message us to begin."  ·  "DM to order."
- Never: "BUY NOW", "Shop the link!!!", "DON'T MISS OUT".

## Quick yes / no
| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|------|---------|
| "Say hello to Noor. Now available." | "😍 Our STUNNING new Noor is here!! Don't miss out 🔥🔥" |
| "Made by hand, with care." | "Each exquisite piece is meticulously handcrafted by our team of artisans over many hours…" |
| "You asked — Noor is back." | "IT'S BACK!!! You asked, we delivered 🙌" |

## Human approval
AI may **draft** captions; a person **reviews and posts**. Posting is public publishing — it
goes through you (or whoever you delegate), never auto-published. See `social-scheduling.md`.
