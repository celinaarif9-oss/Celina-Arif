# WeTarseel onboarding checklist (WhatsApp BSP)

A plain checklist to take you and Pitch from "interested" to a live shared inbox — safely.
Owners: 🟣 **You (founder)** · 🟢 **Pitch**.

> 🔒 Signing up **spends money and touches the live customer line** — founder approves before
> committing. AI stays in **draft mode**; a person sends.

## Step 1 — Questions to ask WeTarseel BEFORE you pay
Confirm it does what we need (don't assume the website):

1. **Coexistence** — "Do you support Meta **Coexistence** so we keep our **existing number**,
   keep the **WhatsApp Business app active** on our phone, and **sync past chats**? How many
   months of history transfer?"
2. **Pricing (PKR)** — monthly plan price for a **multi-agent shared inbox**; any **setup fee**;
   **contract length**; what's included vs **Meta conversation charges** (should be passed
   through **at actuals, no markup**).
3. **Agent seats** — how many logins included? Can we add **founder + Pitch's team**? Roles/
   permissions (so Pitch sees chats but you control settings)?
4. **Shopify** — does it send **order updates / tracking** and **abandoned-cart on WhatsApp**?
   Included or extra?
5. **AI / chatbot** — can the AI answer FAQs in **suggest/draft mode (agent approves before
   send)**, NOT auto-reply? Can we load **our own FAQ**?
6. **Instagram** — can it manage **Instagram DMs in the same inbox** too? (nice-to-have)
7. **Onboarding help** — do they assist with **Meta business verification**, **display-name
   approval**, and enabling Coexistence? Typical **timeline**?
8. **Green tick** (official business account) — do they help apply?
9. **Data & exit** — where is chat data stored, who can access it, can we **export/delete**, and
   what happens to our number/data if we leave?
10. **Support** — hours, channel (WhatsApp/phone), Urdu + English.
