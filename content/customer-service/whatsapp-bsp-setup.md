## The key to keeping everything: Coexistence mode (Meta, 2026)
Don't do the old "delete the number and migrate" — that **loses your chat history**. Instead use
**Coexistence**, which runs the **WhatsApp Business app *and* the API on the same number at the
same time**:
- **Pitch's phone keeps working** (manual chats) **and** you get the shared API inbox. No "one
  or the other."
- **Chats are kept** — up to **6 months of 1:1 history syncs** into the new inbox; app history
  stays on the phone. New messages sync both ways.
- **Same number** — no brand disruption.
- Caveats: the Business app must stay installed on a phone; **group chats don't sync**.
