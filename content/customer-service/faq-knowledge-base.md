## How to order / made-to-order
**Q: How do I order?**
> Each piece is made to order, just for you. Tell us the design and your size, and we'll begin. You can order on celinaarif.com or message us here.

**Q: Is everything made to order?**
> Yes — every piece is made for you once you order, in your size.

## Sizing & measurements
**Q: How does sizing work? / Can I give my measurements?**
> We make to measure. Standard sizes are XXS–XXL, and you can add your own measurements at checkout for a custom fit. Happy to guide you if you're unsure.

**Q: Where do I add custom measurements?**
> At checkout you'll see "Add custom measurements" — pop them in there, and we'll tailor to you.

## Lead time
**Q: How long will my order take?**
> Because each piece is made to order, the making time is: **Luxury Pret — 10–15 working days**,
> **Semi-formals — 15–20 working days**, plus delivery. We'll keep you posted along the way.

## Shipping
**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
> Yes — worldwide, via DHL. Shipping is calculated at checkout by destination and weight.

**Q: How much is delivery?**
> Within Pakistan, a flat **Rs.500**. International is by destination, shown at checkout.

**Q: Will I pay customs/duties? (international)**
> International orders may have **import duties/taxes** set by your country, collected by DHL on delivery. These are separate from our charges.

## Payment
**Q: How can I pay?**
> Card (via our secure checkout), **bank transfer**, or **Western Union**. For bank transfer or Western Union, we'll share the details and confirm once payment is received.

## Order status & tracking
**Q: Where is my order?**
> Let me check for you — could you share your order number? Once it ships you'll get a DHL/courier tracking link by email.

## Customisation
**Q: Can I customise a design (colour/sleeves/length)?**
> The customisation options for each piece are noted in its **description on the website** — take
> a look, and message us if you'd like a hand.

## Exchanges & returns
**Q: Can I return or exchange?**
> As each piece is made to order especially for you, we're unable to offer **returns or
> exchanges**. You'll find our full policy on the website — and of course, we're always here to
> help you choose the right size before you order.

## Care — "Essentials for Care"
**Q: How do I care for my outfit?**
> - Dry clean only
> - Handle with care
> - Do not use bleach or harsh detergents
> - Steam on low heat; avoid ironing directly on embellishments
> - Store in a cool, dry place
> - Keep away from moisture and perfumes
