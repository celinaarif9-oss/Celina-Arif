# Quick replies — paste-ready (WhatsApp + Instagram)

Each block = a **shortcut** + the **message** to paste. Same set works in WhatsApp Business
(Quick replies) and Instagram (Saved replies). Edit the wording anytime.

> 🔒 These are drafts your team **sends** — review before sending. For complaints, refunds,
> delays or custom pricing, **don't use a template — handle it personally.**

---

**/hello**
Hi, thank you for reaching out to Celina Arif. How can we help you today?

**/order**
Every piece is made to order, just for you. Share the design and your size and we'll begin — or order anytime at celinaarif.com.

**/sizing**
We make to measure. Standard sizes are XXS–XXL, and you can add your own measurements at checkout for a custom fit. Happy to help if you're unsure.

**/leadtime**
As each piece is made to order: Luxury Pret takes 10–15 working days and semi-formals 15–20 working days, plus delivery. We'll keep you posted along the way.

**/shipping**
We ship worldwide via DHL. Within Pakistan it's a flat Rs.500; international is calculated at checkout by destination.

**/payment**
You can pay by card at checkout, bank transfer, or Western Union. For bank transfer or Western Union we'll share the details and confirm once received.

**/tracking**
Of course — could you share your order number? Once your order ships you'll receive a tracking link by email.

**/custom**
The customisation options for each piece are noted in its description on the website — take a look, and message us if you'd like a hand.

**/returns**
As each piece is made to order especially for you, we're unable to offer returns or exchanges. Our full policy is on the website — and we're always here to help you choose the right size before ordering.

**/care**
Essentials for care:
• Dry clean only
• Handle with care
• No bleach or harsh detergents
• Steam on low heat; avoid ironing directly on embellishments
• Store in a cool, dry place
• Keep away from moisture and perfumes
