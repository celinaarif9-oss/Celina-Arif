# Celina Arif — sizing & custom measurements (reference)

Source of truth for the customisation options (item 1). **All measurements in inches.**

> The authoritative size chart is the brand's official chart image (provided by the founder).
> Upload that image to the product page as the size guide — it is not transcribed here on
> purpose, to avoid introducing measurement errors. This file captures the *structure* the
> options must cover.

## Standard size range

**XXS · XS · S · M · L · XL · XXL**

Garment types covered by the official chart (each lists its own measurement set):

| Garment type | Measurements on the chart |

Body measurement points on the chart diagram: Shoulder, Chest, Waist, Hip, Thigh, Knee,
Length, Bottom.

## Custom measurements form (made-to-measure)

For customers ordering made-to-measure, capture these **17 fields** (1–16 numeric in inches,
17 free text):

1. Height
2. Shoulder
3. Armhole
4. Arm/Sleeve length
5. Wrist
6. Bicep
7. Bust
8. Under bust
9. High waist
10. Waist
11. Low waist
12. Hip
13. Thigh
14. Ankle
15. Inseam
16. Trouser / Shalwar / Lehenga / Saree / Kaftaan length
17. Any extra requirements

Plus an optional **measurement photo** upload (replaces the current "photo attached in
comments" workaround).
